ScholarXIV offers three subscription tiers designed to meet different research needs, from casual exploration to advanced academic research. All subscriptions are managed through Polar.

## Available Plans

ScholarXIV provides three subscription options:

### Free Plan - $0/month

Perfect for trying out basic features and light research use.

**Key Features:**
- Limited research capabilities
- Unlimited bookmarks
- Basic model selection
- Basic search functionality
- 5 selected papers at once
- 5 references per query
- 1 file upload per research
- BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) support

### Plus Plan - $15/month

<Note>
Recommended for everyday research use
</Note>

Ideal for regular researchers who need more powerful features.

**Key Features:**
- **Everything in Free, plus:**
- Unlimited research
- Global AI chat
- Advanced search capabilities
- Unlimited comments
- Wider model selection
- 30 selected papers at once
- 20 references per query
- Limited file uploads per research

### Pro Plan - $30/month

The ultimate plan for advanced researchers and power users.

**Key Features:**
- **Everything in Plus, plus:**
- Deep research capabilities
- Unlimited file uploads
- Advanced model selection
- 50 selected papers at once
- 50 references per query
- Watermark-free PDF exports
- View generated PDFs in-app

<Info>
Additional usage limits may apply depending on the AI model and provider you select.
</Info>

## Choosing a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to ScholarXIV">
    If you haven't already, [sign in](/guide/authentication) to your ScholarXIV account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to the pricing page">
    During checkout after your first sign-in, or visit the [pricing page](https://scholarxiv.com/pricing) anytime to view available plans.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your plan">
    Click "Get Started" on the plan you want to subscribe to. Free plan users can click "Try ScholarXIV" to get started immediately.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be redirected to Polar's secure checkout page to complete your subscription (Plus and Pro plans only).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Subscription

### Accessing the Customer Portal

ScholarXIV uses Polar for subscription management. You can manage your subscription through the Polar Customer Portal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile menu">
    Click on your profile avatar in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Customer Portal'">
    Select the "Customer Portal" option from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage your subscription">
    In the Polar portal, you can:
    - View your current plan
    - Update payment methods
    - View billing history
    - Cancel your subscription
    - Download invoices
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a higher tier:

1. Click your profile avatar in the sidebar
2. Select "Upgrade Plan" from the dropdown
3. Choose your desired plan on the pricing page
4. Complete the checkout process

<Info>
When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing period.
</Info>

### Downgrading Your Plan

To downgrade to a lower tier:

1. Access the **Customer Portal** from your profile menu
2. Navigate to your subscription settings
3. Select the plan you want to downgrade to
4. Confirm the change

<Warning>
Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll retain access to your current plan's features until then.
</Warning>

### Canceling Your Subscription

1. Open the **Customer Portal** from your profile menu
2. Navigate to subscription settings
3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm the cancellation

<Note>
After cancellation, you'll be automatically moved to the Free plan and retain access to basic features.
</Note>

## Billing and Payment

### Payment Methods

Polar accepts the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Other payment methods supported by Stripe

### Billing Cycle

- Subscriptions are billed **monthly**
- Billing occurs on the same day each month as your initial subscription
- You'll receive an email receipt after each payment

### Discount Codes

ScholarXIV supports discount codes during checkout:

1. During the checkout process, look for the "Discount Code" field
2. Enter your code
3. Click "Apply" to see the discounted price

## Plan Limits

Your current plan determines various usage limits:

### Selected Papers Limit

- **Free**: 5 papers at once
- **Plus**: 30 papers at once
- **Pro**: 50 papers at once

This limit controls how many papers you can select simultaneously for research analysis.

### References Per Query

- **Free**: 5 references
- **Plus**: 20 references
- **Pro**: 50 references

This determines how many reference papers the AI can cite when answering your research questions.

### File Uploads

- **Free**: 1 file per research
- **Plus**: Limited files per research
- **Pro**: Unlimited files per research

Upload PDFs and other documents to enhance your research context.

<Warning>
If you reach your plan's limits, you'll need to either upgrade your plan or wait until your selection/query is complete before starting new ones.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Can I switch between plans?">
  Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time through the Customer Portal. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I cancel my subscription?">
  If you cancel a paid subscription, you'll be moved to the Free plan at the end of your billing period. You'll retain access to your bookmarks, collections, and basic features, but lose access to premium features like advanced search, unlimited research, and expanded AI capabilities.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
  Refund policies are managed through Polar. Contact support through the Customer Portal if you believe you're eligible for a refund.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)?">
  BYOK allows you to use your own API keys for AI providers (like OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) instead of relying on ScholarXIV's shared quota. This is available on all plans.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there academic or institutional discounts?">
  Contact the ScholarXIV team through the [feedback portal](https://scholarxiv.featurebase.app/) to inquire about academic or institutional pricing.
</Accordion>
