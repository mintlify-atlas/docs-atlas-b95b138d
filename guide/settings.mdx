ScholarXIV offers various settings and preferences to customize your research experience. This guide covers account management, appearance settings, and other preferences.

## Account Settings

### Viewing Your Account Information

You can view your account details from the profile menu:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the profile menu">
    Click on your profile avatar in the sidebar. This is typically located at the bottom or top of the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View account details">
    The dropdown menu displays:
    - Your profile picture
    - Display name
    - Email address
    - Current subscription plan (Free, Plus, or Pro)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your display name and profile picture are pulled from your authentication provider (Google, GitHub, Twitter, or Hugging Face) and cannot be changed directly in ScholarXIV.
</Info>

### Managing Your Profile

To update your profile information:

1. **Profile Picture**: Update your avatar on your authentication provider (Google, GitHub, etc.)
2. **Display Name**: Change your name in your authentication provider settings
3. **Email Address**: Update your email through your authentication provider

<Note>
Changes to your profile on the authentication provider may take a few minutes to sync with ScholarXIV. Sign out and sign back in to force a refresh.
</Note>

## Appearance Settings

### Theme Selection

ScholarXIV supports both light and dark themes to match your preference and reduce eye strain.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the profile menu">
    Click on your profile avatar in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle theme">
    Click "Toggle Theme" from the dropdown menu to switch between light and dark mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Theme Features:**
- **Light Mode**: Bright, clean interface ideal for well-lit environments
- **Dark Mode**: Reduced brightness for comfortable reading in low-light conditions
- **Persistent**: Your theme preference is saved and persists across sessions

<Info>
The theme preference is stored in your browser's local storage and as a cookie, so it will be remembered when you return to ScholarXIV.
</Info>

## Subscription Management

### Viewing Your Current Plan

Your current subscription plan is always visible in your profile menu:

- **Free Plan**: Standard display
- **Plus Plan**: Shows with an "Upgrade" button if you're not on Pro
- **Pro Plan**: Highlighted with a purple badge

### Upgrading Your Plan

From the profile menu:

1. If you're on Free or Plus, you'll see an "Upgrade" button next to your profile
2. Click "Upgrade" or select "Upgrade Plan" from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be taken to the pricing page to select a new plan

<Note>
See the [Subscription Plans](/guide/subscriptions) guide for detailed information on plan features and pricing.
</Note>

### Accessing the Customer Portal

Manage your subscription, billing, and payment methods:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open profile menu">
    Click on your profile avatar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Customer Portal'">
    Select the "Customer Portal" option with the wallet icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage subscription">
    In the Polar customer portal, you can:
    - Update payment methods
    - View billing history
    - Download invoices
    - Manage your subscription
    - Cancel your subscription
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Privacy & Security

### Authentication Security

ScholarXIV uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authentication:

- **No password storage**: ScholarXIV never stores your passwords
- **Secure sessions**: Sessions use encrypted tokens
- **Provider-managed security**: Your credentials are managed by trusted providers (Google, GitHub, etc.)

### Session Security

- Sessions expire after **7 days** of inactivity
- Sessions refresh every **24 hours** when you're active
- Sign out from any device to immediately terminate your session

### Data Privacy

ScholarXIV requests minimal permissions from authentication providers:

- Basic profile information (name, profile picture)
- Email address
- **No access** to your repositories, posts, or other private data

<Warning>
Always sign out from shared or public computers to protect your account.
</Warning>

## Usage Tracking

ScholarXIV tracks certain usage events to improve the platform:

- **Login/Logout events**: Tracked with timestamps for analytics
- **Feature usage**: Understanding which features are most valuable
- **Plan limits**: Monitoring to ensure you stay within your subscription limits

<Note>
Usage tracking is anonymized and used solely for improving the platform. See the privacy policy for details.
</Note>

## Account Actions

### Signing Out

To end your current session:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open profile menu">
    Click your profile avatar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Logout">
    Select the "Logout" option (red text with logout icon).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm sign-out">
    You'll be signed out and redirected to the landing page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Deletion

Currently, account deletion must be requested through the Polar Customer Portal or by contacting support through the [Feedback Portal](https://scholarxiv.featurebase.app/).

<Warning>
Deleting your account is permanent and will remove all your research, bookmarks, collections, and preferences.
</Warning>

## Browser Settings

### Required Browser Permissions

For ScholarXIV to work properly, ensure your browser allows:

- **Cookies**: Required for authentication and theme preferences
- **JavaScript**: Required for the application to function
- **Local Storage**: Used to store theme preferences

### Recommended Browser Settings

- **Clear cookies on exit**: Disable this if you want to stay signed in
- **Third-party cookies**: May be required for OAuth authentication
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled

<Info>
ScholarXIV works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="My theme keeps resetting">
  If your theme preference doesn't persist:
  
  1. Check if your browser is set to clear cookies on exit
  2. Ensure local storage is enabled
  3. Try a different browser to isolate the issue
  4. Check if you're in private/incognito mode (settings won't persist)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I can't access the Customer Portal">
  If you're having trouble accessing the Polar Customer Portal:
  
  1. Ensure you're signed in to ScholarXIV
  2. Make sure you have an active subscription (Free plan users have limited portal access)
  3. Check if pop-up blockers are preventing the portal from opening
  4. Try accessing it directly from the profile menu
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My profile picture isn't updating">
  Profile pictures are synced from your authentication provider:
  
  1. Update your avatar on your provider (Google, GitHub, etc.)
  2. Wait a few minutes for the change to propagate
  3. Sign out of ScholarXIV and sign back in
  4. Clear your browser cache if the old image persists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I change my email address?">
  Email addresses are managed by your authentication provider:
  
  1. Update your email on your authentication provider (Google, GitHub, etc.)
  2. Sign out of ScholarXIV
  3. Sign back in - your new email should appear
  
  Note: If you want to use a completely different authentication provider, you'll need to create a new ScholarXIV account.
</Accordion>
