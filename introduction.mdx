## Welcome to ScholarXIV

ScholarXIV is a modern, AI-powered academic research platform that transforms how you discover, analyze, and interact with research papers from arXiv. Whether you're a researcher, student, or academic enthusiast, ScholarXIV provides intelligent tools to accelerate your research workflow.

## What makes ScholarXIV different

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="sparkles" title="Context-aware AI conversations">
    Unlike traditional search tools, ScholarXIV's AI assistant understands the context of research papers. Select multiple papers and chat about them together, compare methodologies, or synthesize findings across different studies.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="users" title="Community discussions">
    View and participate in discussions about papers. See what other researchers are saying, share insights, and engage with the academic community directly on the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="layer-group" title="Multi-paper analysis">
    Select and analyze multiple papers in a single chat session. Perfect for literature reviews, comparing approaches, or understanding how research evolves over time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="code" title="Open source">
    ScholarXIV is open source and can be self-hosted. You have full control over your data and can customize the platform to fit your research needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technology stack

ScholarXIV is built with modern web technologies to deliver a fast, responsive experience:

- **Frontend**: SvelteKit 5 with TypeScript for a reactive, performant UI
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4 for modern, customizable design
- **Database**: MongoDB for flexible data storage
- **AI**: Google Gemini for intelligent paper analysis and chat
- **Authentication**: Better Auth with support for Google, GitHub, Twitter, and Hugging Face
- **Subscriptions**: Polar integration for flexible pricing tiers
